About DramValue

Whisky secondary market pricing, visible to everyone. Not just dealers and collectors who've been in the game long enough to know.

What We Do

DramValue tracks whisky prices across the secondary market: major auction houses, retail platforms, and valuation services. We aggregate data from multiple sources so you can see what a bottle actually sold for, not just what someone hopes to get for it.

Whether you're checking a bid before an auction closes, researching a bottle at a bar, or keeping tabs on a collection, the data is here and it's free.
