Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What We Collect

For visitors browsing without an account, we collect no personal information. Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) may be retained temporarily for security and performance purposes.

For registered accounts, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We do not collect payment information.

How We Use It

  • Email: to send price alert notifications you explicitly request
  • Account data: to maintain your saved collections and alerts
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

Cookies

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in. No third-party tracking cookies. No analytics cookies beyond our own server logs.

Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion by contacting us (see the About page). Server logs are rotated within 90 days.

Third-Party Data

Price data shown on this site is aggregated from public auction and retail sources. DramValue does not share your personal data with those sources.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted on this page with an updated date.
